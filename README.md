AI Legal AdvisorOverviewAI Legal Advisor is an app that provides users with legal advice for their legal issues. The app aims to offer accessible and user-friendly guidance on various legal matters.

Features
- Get legal advice on a range of topics
- User-friendly interface for easy navigation
- Powered by AI for efficient responses

How to Use
1. Open the app and describe your legal issue
2. Receive advice based on your input
3. Follow up with further questions if needed
